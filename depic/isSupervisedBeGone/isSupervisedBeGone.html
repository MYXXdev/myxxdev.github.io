A one click solution to remove the "is supervised" message
in Settings AFTER installing MYbloXX for iOS.

NOTE: PLEASE ENSURE YOU'RE INSTALLING THIS
AFTER YOU'VE INSTALLED THE PROFILE!

Installing prior to adding the profile/alongside the MYbloXX for iOS package will cause the profile installation to fail!
